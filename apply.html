Elgin Technology Center Membership Application

The Elgin Technology Center (ETC) is a collaborative community, in the suburbs, where entrepreneurs and small businesses grow and evolve.

If you're an entrepreneur or small business owner looking for flexible, affordable coworking space then ETC may be what you're looking for. Membership is only $100 per month. Members may also rent private offices or open offices for an additional small investment.

Benefits of Membership:
24/7 access to ETC
Collaboration opportunities
Mentorship
Open coworking
Printing and copying
Wireless high-speed internet
Access to receive mail and packages
Access to rent open or private offices
Access to ETC workshops and programming
Access to conference rooms in the Shared coworking areas
