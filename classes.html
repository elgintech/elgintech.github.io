    FAQ's

    Is this class only for people who want to launch a startup?

    Don't let the word "startup" fool you. The lean startup methodology can be applied to any new business or product.

    Why are we not learning how to write a business plan?

    Business plans take a lot of time to write, and are meant to be structured around a validated product. Most ideas never survive first contact with the customer. You will be ready for an actual business plan once you have validated your product and business model using the lean startup process.

    Do I really need to have an actual business or product idea of my own to use in this class?

    Yes, you must have a business idea or product that you want to validate or launch. This class is designed around the concept of "learn by doing." Each week you will apply what is learned in the classroom to your own idea.
