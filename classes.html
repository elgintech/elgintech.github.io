  • Lean Startup 101 NEW

    Class Summary
    In 8 weeks you will learn about the lean startup methodology, and apply what you learn weekly in the development of your own business, product, or idea. We will show you how to "get out of the building" in order to validate your solution, customer base, and revenue model with the least amount of risk. By the end of this class you will have the knowledge needed to launch and evolve a business, while giving yourself the best chance of achieving success.
    Schedule
    Wed. evenings April 2 - May 21, 6-8PM.
    Requirements
    The requirements for this class are simple. You must have an idea that you are passionate about launching, and you are ready to work hard validating it in 8 weeks.
    What you will learn
    Creating a lean canvas
    Developing hypotheses
    Risk assessment
    Design of experiments
    Customer development
    Problem / Solution validation
    Revenue model validation
    MVP design and validation
    Customer cycle
    Product / Market fit
    FAQ's

    Is this class only for people who want to launch a startup?

    Don't let the word "startup" fool you. The lean startup methodology can be applied to any new business or product.
